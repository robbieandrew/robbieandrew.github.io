New vehicle sales

Norway has experienced the earliest transition of its passenger car fleet to battery-electric vehicles, with strong government support and wide public acceptance. Factors that have contributed: lack of domestic car manufacturing industry, long history of renewable electricity generation, early introduction of incentives, high taxes on polluting vehicles, etc.

The figures here demonstrate this transition by showing the share of new passenger-car sales by drivetrain, with battery-electric in blue increasing from very low levels in 2010 to about 90% in 2024. The data can be downloaded from the link at the bottom of the page.
