Country energy and emissions charts

What is this page?

On this page you'll find a collection of charts presenting data for a single country, where the charts available vary by country. In many cases, charts showing the officially estimated emissions as reported to the UNFCCC are present, and the number of these is increasing as more developing countries report under the Biennial Transparency Report (BTR) system. These official reports are separate for "Annex 1" ("developed") countries and "Non-Annex 1" ("developing" countries. Since Annex 1 countries have been reporting for many years, revising their entire data series every year, I show these revisions over time.

Other charts here are often just those showing data that I have worked on in the course of other projects and have decided to share here to supplement country-level information.
