Emissions Projections for 2020

Global fossil CO2 emissions are projected to decline by about 7% in 2020, based on the median of four different estimates.

The 2020 projections are based on preliminary data and modelling, and are the median of the four studies.
Sources: CDIAC; Friedlingstein et al 2020; Global Carbon Budget 2020

Consumption-based emissions (carbon footprint)

Allocating fossil CO2 emissions to consumption provides an alternative perspective.
USA and EU28 are net importers of embodied emissions, China and India are net exporters.

Consumption-based emissions are calculated by adjusting the standard production-based emissions to account for international trade
Source: Peters et al 2011; Friedlingstein et al 2020; Global Carbon Project 2019
